## Idea
Problem:

India has over 50 million pending court cases. Yet the deeper crisis is invisible — the millions of citizens who never even reach the court because they don’t know their rights exist.

A daily wage worker whose employer refuses to pay. A tenant whose landlord illegally withholds a security deposit. A farmer cheated by a local trader. A woman facing workplace harassment. Every one of them has legal protection under Indian law. Most will never use it — not because the law doesn’t protect them, but because they have no way to access it.

Legal aid in India is broken at the entry point. A lawyer consultation costs ₹2,000–₹10,000 minimum. Government legal aid cells are understaffed, underpromoted, and geographically out of reach for rural citizens. Online resources are written in English legalese that requires a law degree to interpret. The result: justice is effectively reserved for those who can afford it

Solution: VIDHI

VIDHI is an AI-powered legal rights assistant that bridges the gap between a citizen’s problem and their legal options — in plain language, in seconds, at zero cost.

A user describes their situation in natural language. VIDHI identifies the exact applicable Indian laws and sections, drafts a ready-to-send legal notice, identifies the correct court or authority to approach, and outlines the next three actionable steps — all within 15 seconds.

Impact:

One tool. Every Indian. Every legal problem.
